# Privacy Policy for Mind Yourself Journal

  **Last updated:** January 2026

  ## The Short Version

  Mind Yourself Journal stores everything locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no way to access your data.

  ## What the App Stores (On Your Device Only)

  Mind Yourself Journal keeps the following data on your phone:

  - Journal entries (check-ins, moods, gratitudes, reflections)
  - Habits you create and track
  - Goals and milestones
  - Health data from Apple Health (with your permission)

  **This data never leaves your device.**

  ## What We Don't Do

  - We don't have servers or cloud storage
  - We don't collect personal information
  - We don't track your location
  - We don't use analytics
  - We don't show ads
  - We can't see or access any of your data

  ## Apple Health (HealthKit)

  If you grant permission, the app reads health data (steps, distance, calories, active minutes) to track fitness goals. This data:

  - Stays on your device
  - Is never shared with anyone
  - Can be revoked anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

  ## Deleting Your Data

  Simply delete the app. Since everything is stored locally, removing the app deletes all your data permanently.
