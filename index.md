# Privacy Policy for Mind Yourself

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Mind Yourself is a personal wellness app that helps you track habits, journal your thoughts, and monitor your progress. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
- **Journal entries:** Your morning and evening check-ins, moods, gratitudes, and reflections
- **Habits:** The habits you create and track
- **Goals:** Your personal goals and milestones
- **Health data:** With your permission, step count, distance, calories, and active minutes from Apple Health

### What We Don't Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not show advertisements

## Data Storage

**All your data stays on your device.** Mind Yourself does not have servers or cloud storage. Your information is stored locally using Apple's secure on-device database.

- We cannot see your data
- We cannot access your data
- Your data is never transmitted over the internet

## HealthKit Data

If you grant permission, Mind Yourself reads health data from Apple HealthKit to help track fitness-related goals. This data:
- Is only read, never written or modified
- Stays on your device
- Is never shared with third parties
- Can be revoked anytime in Settings → Privacy → Health

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Delete the Mind Yourself app from your device

Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app removes everything permanently.

## Children's Privacy

Mind Yourself is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
